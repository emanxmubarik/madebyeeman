About Me

I’m Eman, a journalism student at CCNY with a background in computer science. Through my journalism coursework, I have been able to write stories on a wide range of topics, from homeless shelters to international students. I, also, have experience coding from creating websites to working on developing a web-scraper. I’m also passionate about public service and advocacy. In the future, I hope to pursue a career in law, specializing in international law and human rights, where I can advocate for others and create meaningful impact.

I have become increasingly interested in investigative and data journalism, and using data to highlight systemic issues. I am eager to continue exploring opportunities at the intersection of technology and journalism. Let's connect if you're interested in collaboration, whether that's to investigate community issues or create platforms to advocate for others.

In my free time, I enjoy reading books, thrifting and watching the newest movie with my friend. I'm currently reading Babel by R.F. Kuang and looking forward to The Backrooms movie coming out.
