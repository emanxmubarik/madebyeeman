About Me

I’m Eman, a journalism student at The City College of New York (CCNY) with a background in computer science. I have experience writing stories that highlight community issues and building coding projects, including web scrapers and websites.

I’m especially interested in storytelling through investigative and data journalism, using data to uncover patterns, trends, and systemic issues. I’m continuing to develop my skills in data-driven analysis and reporting.

I’m also passionate about public service and advocacy. In the future, I hope to pursue a career in law, specializing in international law and human rights, where I can advocate for others and create meaningful impact.
